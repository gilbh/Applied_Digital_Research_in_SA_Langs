ṛṣaya ūcuḥ || 
katham liṅgam pratiṣṭhāpyam katham vā tasya lakṣaṇam | katham vā tat-sama-bhyarcyam deśe kāle ca kena hi || 1

sūta uvāca ||
yuṣmad-artham pravakṣyāmi buddhi-ātma-vaśānataḥ | anukūle śubhe kāle puṇye tīrthe taṭe tathā || 2

yathā iṣṭam liṅgam āripyam yatra syā-nityam arcanam | pārthivena tathā api enam taijasena yathā āruci || 3

kalā-lakṣaṇa-saṃyuktam liṅgam pūjā-phalam labhet | sarva-lakṣaṇa-saṃyuktam sadyaḥ pūjā-phala-pradam || 4

care viśiṣyate sūkṣmam sthāvare sthūlam eva hi | sa-lakṣaṇam sapīṭham ca sthāpaye nirmitam śivam || 5

maṇḍalam catur-asram vā tri-koṇamathavā tathā | khaṭva-aṅga-vanmadhya-sūkṣmam liṅga-pīṭham mahā-phalam || 6

prathamam mṛcchila-adibhyo liṅgam lohādibhiḥ kṛtam | yena liṅgam tena pīṭham sthāvare hi viśiṣyate || 7

liṅgam pīṭham caer tvekam liṅgam bāṇakṛtam vinā | liṅgapramāṇam kartṝṇām dvādaśāṅgalamuttamam || 8

nyūnam cetphalamalpam syādadhikam naiva duṣyate | karturekāṅgalanyūnam care S pi ca tathaiva hi || 9

ādau vimānam śilpena kārye devagaṇairyutam | tatra garbhagṛhe ramye dṛḍhe darpaṇasannibhe || 10
