avidyā-bhūta-saṃsāre duḥkha-nāśaḥ sukham yataḥ |
jñānāt ādyanta-śūnyam syāt tasmāt ātmā bhavet sukham ||

yasmāt nāśitam ajñānam jñānena viśva-kāraṇam |
tasmāt ātmā bhavet jñānam jnana tasmāt sanātanam ||

kālatḥ vividham viśvam ydā chaiva bhavet idam |
tada ekaḥ asti saḥ eva ātmā kalpanā-apthavrjitaḥ ||

bāhyāni sarva-bhūtāni vināśam yānti kālataḥ |
yato vācho nivartante ātmā dvaitavivarjitaḥ ||

na kham vāyuḥ na cha agniḥ cha na jalam pṛthivī na cha |
na etat kāryam na īśvara-ādi pūrṇa-eka-ātmā bhavet khalu ||

ātmānam ātmanā yogī paśyati ātmani niśchitam |
sarva-saṃkalpa-saṃnyāsī tyakta-mithyā-bhava-ajrahaḥ ||

ātmanā ātmani cha ātmānam dṛṣṭvā anantam sukha-ātmakam |
vismṛtya viśvam ramate samādheḥ tīvrataḥ tathā ||

māyā eva viśva-jananī nāśyā tattva-dhiyā parā |
yadā nāśam samāyāti viśvam na asti tadā khalu ||

heyam sarvam idam yat tu māyā-vilasitam yataḥ |
svatḥ na prīti-viṣayaḥ tanu-vitta-sukha-ātmakaḥ ||
