śikṣām rakṣitu-kāmena cittam rakṣyam prayatnataḥ 
na śikṣā rakṣitum śakyā calam cittam arakṣatā ||1||

adāntāḥ matta-mātaṅgāḥ na kurvanti iha tām vyathām
karoti yām avīci-ādau muktaḥ citta-mataṅgajaḥ ||2||

baddhaḥ cet citta-mātaṅgaḥ smṛti-rajju ivā samantataḥ
bhayam astam gatam sarvam kṛtsnam kalyāṇam āgatam ||3||

vyāghrāḥ siṃhāḥ gajāḥ ṛkṣāḥ sarpāḥ sarve ca śatravaḥ
sarve naraka-pālāḥ ca ḍākinyoḥ rākṣasāḥ tathā ||4||

sarve baddhāḥ bhavanti ete cittasya ekasya bandhanāt
cittasya ekasya damanāt sarve dāntāḥ bhavanti ca ||5||

yasmāt bhayāni sarvāṇi duḥkhāni apramitāni ca
cittāt eva bhavanti iti kathitam tattva-vādinā ||6||
